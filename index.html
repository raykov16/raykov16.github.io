About Squids

At the top of the hierarchy are the square squids, who act as supervisors. They oversee the circles and triangles, giving them instructions and making sure everything runs smoothly. Squares also communicate directly with the Front Man, handling any issues that require leadership and coordination.

The triangle squids have a more active role during the games. Armed with weapons, they are the enforcers who maintain order and deal with any disobedience swiftly. They’re essentially the muscle of the operation, ensuring everything stays under control.

The circle squids are the lowest rank among the pink-hooded enforcers. They handle all the basic tasks, like cleaning the game areas, distributing food, and moving players around. They don’t carry weapons and have no authority, working strictly under orders from those above them.
